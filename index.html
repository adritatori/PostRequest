Welcome to
PostRequest

PostRequest is a secure B2B platform for food and beverage manufacturers to request quotes for equipment, supplies, and services. Vendors registered with PostRequest can also generate quotes and chat directly with buyers.

Why Use
PostRequest?

Food and beverage manufacturers require an extensive variety of resources. The list of needed products can be daunting, from ingredients to manufacturing equipment parts. That's why we are proud to offer PostRequest’s services to help reduce your invaulable time spent searching for quality products and/or potential buyers to ensure your business runs smoothly.
